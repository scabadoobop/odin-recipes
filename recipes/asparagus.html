Roasted Asparagus with Parmesan

Seriously, its just roasted asparagus with parmesan

"Black Magic bars our way, but the Will of a Templar is stronger!" - Kormac, Diablo III

Ingredients!

  • olive oil cooking spray
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, tough ends trimmed
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

Steps to make:

  1. Gather all the ingredients, Mise en place, or something. ha ha, I swear I can cook.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Spray the inside of a 9x13 casserole dish with olive oil cooking spray. Place asparagus in the dish and lightly spray spears with cooking spray.
  4. Sprinkle asparagus with parmesan cheese, sea salt, and garlic powder.
  5. Roast in preheated oven until fork easily punctures thickest part of stem, about 12 minutes.
