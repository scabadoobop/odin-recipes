Quick and Easy Chicken Noodle Soup

Seriously, its just quick and easy chicken noodle soup

“It's dangerous to go alone, take this!” - The Legend of Zelda

Ingredients!

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 oz) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can vegetable broth
  • 1/2 pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps to make:

  1. Gather all the ingredients, Mise en place, or something. ha ha, I swear I can cook.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
